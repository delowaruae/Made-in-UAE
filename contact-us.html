    FAQ
    Frequently Asked Questions
    Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.
    How do I verify a supplier before placing an order? Buyer Guide
    All verified suppliers have completed multi-step verification including business license checks, factory audits, and product certification reviews. Look for the Gold Supplier badge and Trade Assurance seal. You can also request a third-party inspection for orders over $5,000.
    What is Trade Assurance and how does it protect me? Payments
    Trade Assurance holds your payment securely until you confirm receipt of goods matching order specifications. If products don't match, we mediate a resolution or issue a refund. It covers quality issues, delivery delays, and supplier non-compliance.
    How do I become a verified supplier? Suppliers
    Apply through the Supplier Portal with your business registration documents, product certifications, and undergo a factory inspection. The verification process typically takes 5–10 business days, after which you gain access to 40M+ buyers globally.
    What currencies and payment methods do you support? Payments
    We support 30+ currencies and multiple payment methods including T/T wire transfer, letter of credit (L/C), credit card, PayPal, and local payment methods. For orders over $50,000, our finance team can discuss custom payment terms and trade finance options.
    Can I request samples before placing a bulk order? Orders
    Yes, most suppliers offer samples via the product listing page. Sample costs are typically paid by the buyer but may be refunded on the first bulk order. Our team can help negotiate sample terms for large potential orders.
    How do I handle customs and import duties? Logistics
    Import duties vary by country and product category. Use our HS Code Lookup tool and consult a licensed customs broker. We partner with major freight forwarders who can provide door-to-door DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) shipping for a seamless import experience.

    Enterprise & Large Order Support

    Managing orders over $100K? Get a dedicated account manager, priority support, custom payment terms, and access to our exclusive enterprise supplier network.
